Our Story

Born from the
fire of Pampanga

Koyang Ry Sizzling House started as a simple dream — to bring the pleasure of a real steak to every hardworking person in Angeles City. We planted our flag on Friendship Highway, where the community lives, works, and eats.

Pampanga has always been the culinary heart of the Philippines. We honor that by doing what Kapampangans do best: cooking with passion, serving with pride, and making sure no one leaves the table hungry.

From sizzling steaks to waffles, from bone marrow bulalo to crispy sisig — we are everything you crave, all day and all night.
